KaTeX Math Rendering Demo

Inline Math

The quadratic formula is $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$ and it's very useful.

Einstein's famous equation $E = mc^2$ shows the mass-energy equivalence.

The integral $\int_0^1 x^2 dx = \frac{1}{3}$ is a basic calculus example.

Display Math (Block)

The Fourier transform:

$$\hat{f}(\xi) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x) e^{-2\pi i x \xi} dx$$

Matrix multiplication:

$$\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} e & f \\ g & h \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} ae + bg & af + bh \\ ce + dg & cf + dh \end{pmatrix}$$

A complex integral:

$$\oint_C \frac{f(z)}{z-a} dz = 2\pi i \cdot f(a)$$

This is Cauchy's integral formula.
